About Lorna!

While I start out pretty quiet a shy, I'm actually a rather outgoing person. I'm an extroverted introvert! I'm double majoring, president of the EWU Gamers Club, working at the EWU Bookstore, and living my life as best as I can. I've had a passion for art since I was in elementary school and developed a major interest in graphic design when I was a freshman in high school. It's something that I have a deep passion for, so be prepared to watch me get extremely excited! Aside from everything else, I love games. Gaming is another thing that I have done since I was in elementary school and my favorite hobby has only grown over the past few years. I definitely like hanging out with friends on a Friday evening playing Magic: The Gathering or Super Smash Bros.
